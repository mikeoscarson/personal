Queso Dip

Ingredients

  1. 1 lb Ground Beef
  2. 1 Box Velveeta Cheese
  3. 1 Can Rotel Tomatoes with Green Chili
  4. 1 Can Green Chili

Steps

  1. Brown meat and drain off grease
  2. Mix in Rotel Sauce and Green Chili
  3. Heat through
  4. Melt cheese in microwave for several minutes. Do not let it brown
  5. Add cheese to pot and mix all
  6. When hot, transfer to crock pot on low
