Grilled Salmon

Ingredients

  1. Salmon
  2. Marinade, such as
    • Lawry's Honey Bourbon
    • Newman's Own Sesame Ginger

Steps

  1. Marinade in fridge for 1 hour minimim
  2. Spray grill with anti-stick Pam or other
  3. Preheat grill to 450 degrees
  4. Grill at 450, starting with skin-side down
  5. Done when internal temperature reaches 130 degrees
