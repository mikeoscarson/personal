Smoked Brisket

Ingredients

  1. Swanson's Beef Broth (no msg)
  2. French's Classic Yellow Mustard
  3. Rub: mix equal parts of the following
    1. Montreal Steak Seasoning
    2. Mrs. Dash
    3. Salt
    4. Pepper
    5. Morton's Season Salt

Steps

  1. Rub liberally with mustard
  2. Apply rub to all sides
  3. Smoke at 250 degrees until internal temp reaches 165 degrees
  4. Wrap in foil and continue smoking until internal temperature is 205 degrees
  5. Remove from smoker and let rest for 1 hour, preferably in a cooler
  6. Slice across grain and serve
