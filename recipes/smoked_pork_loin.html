Smoked Pork Loin

Ingredients for Rub

  • One Tablespoon of each of the following:
    1. Garlic Powder
    2. Paprika
    3. Onion Powder
    4. Cumin
    5. Mrs. Dash
    6. Grill Mates Brown Sugar Bourbon
    7. Grill Mates Montreal Steak Seasoning
    8. Kosher Salt
    9. Pepper
  • Brown sugar: amount equal to all of the other ingredients

Steps

  1. Inject loins with apple juice
  2. Apply rub liberally to pork loin
  3. Smoke at 225 degrees until internal temp is 145 degrees
